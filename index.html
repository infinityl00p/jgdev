About Portfolio

Born and raised in beautiful British Columbia, I am inherently adventurous, curious and full of ideas. All of these ideas translate into my passion for building applications and websites in Javascript, React, Node or the best tools to get the job done.

I don't subscribe to the idea that there is only one way of building something. I like to weigh the cost/benefit of a certain solution problem and try to think of any possible alternates. Of course, being a computer science graduate has helped me refine this skill. Not only does it translate well to development, it translates to all facets of my life.

Web development is in my nature. I feel blessed to have chosen a field where I can bring my ideas to life at anytime. I am excited for the future of technology and I am even more excited for the role that I will play in it. Of course, it is humbling to remember that it all started with a "Hello World"... :)
